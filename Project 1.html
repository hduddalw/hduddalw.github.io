ABOUT


Harshal Duddalwar is a designer from India, currently pursuing his MFA in Graphic Design at the Rhode Island School of Design, PVD, RI.
Intrigued by optimal, functional, yet emotive visuals, he takes a research-led and process-driven approach to his work. Interested in typography, layouts, brands, and philosophies, besides social design, his practice focuses on branding, editorial, system, and experience design.
Previously worked at Leaf, Microsoft, Animal, The Minimalist, and Cartoon Mango. View resume here.
